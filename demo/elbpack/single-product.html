Vintage Leather Backpack

A perfect blend of style and functionality, our Classic Leather Backpack is designed for professionals and travelers. Crafted from premium full-grain leather, it's durable, water-resistant, and ideal for daily use or weekend trips.

  • Material: Full-grain, water-resistant leather for durability and elegance.
  • Size: 16" x 12" x 6", offering 20 liters of storage space.
  • Weight: Lightweight at 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) for easy carrying.
  • Compartments: Padded laptop sleeve (fits up to 15”), water bottle pocket, and multiple organizational pockets.
  • Durability: Reinforced stitching, heavy-duty zippers, and padded adjustable straps for comfort.
Benefits:
  • Comfortable & Stylish: Ergonomic design with padded straps.
